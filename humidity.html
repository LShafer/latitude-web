Humidity vs. Latitude



Humidity appears to have a slight correlation to latitude, as the plot shows that humidity tends to increase as latitude increases north of the equator. Interestingly, these numbers correspond to summer in the Northern Hemisphere, so further research could evaluate the same data during summer in the Southern Hemisphere to determine if the same correlation exists.
